2019 · Project

Avatars for Two Billion.

How do you create a likeness system for billions of unique and diverse people?

TL;DR

We built Facebook Avatars. The bulk of the work was not pixels, but decisions, strategy and representing our diversity.

Building a large system was hard, required more time and people than expected. Vision and repitition are important to align the team and stakeholders.

The Opportunity

In a world of increasingly less social brodcast sharing and the move to private sharing as an overall trend giving people the ability to communicate in a way that feels emotive and personalized becomes increasingly important.

For many people Facebook (and it's extended ecosystem–IG, Whatsapp, etc) is their online identity, yet this ecosystem lacks personalization that competitors like Snapchat have.

Through research we validated that, on average, people felt that custom stickers felt more personally expressive than a sticker pack or emojis and felt that they could get across what they meant better by using them.
